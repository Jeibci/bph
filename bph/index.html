Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome! Here, we address some common questions to help you better understand our hub, our programs, and what you can expect from enrolling with us.

Is the hub registered and recognized?

Yes, our hub is fully registered and recognized. We adhere to industry standards to ensure the best educational experience for our students.

Is my money safe, and is there a guarantee?

Absolutely. We prioritize the security of your investment and uphold a transparent and reliable process to guarantee that your funds are safe with us.

What are the modes of the classes?

All classes are conducted strictly online. This remote setup provides flexibility and accessibility, enabling you to learn from anywhere.

What will students receive after enrolling?

Enrolled students will receive comprehensive class materials, access to class recordings, and a certificate of excellence or completion upon finishing the program.

Will students receive a certification after completing the program?

Yes, upon successful completion of the program, students are awarded a certificate that recognizes their achievement.

How many times am I allowed to pay for the course?

You can pay in 2 or 3 installments, depending on the course you choose. The option available will be communicated during the enrollment process.
